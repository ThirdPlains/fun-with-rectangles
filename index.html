How To Play

  • You can drag a rectangle by holding down the mouse button.

  • The up and down arrow keys can be used to scale the first rectangle. The left and right keys can be used to make it rotate.

  • If you feel like changing the color of the rectangle, you can do so by clicking various keys. Such as: 'o' - for orange, 'r' - for red, 'g' - for green, 'b' - for blue, 'p' - for purple, 'y' - for yellow, and 'w' for white.

  • You can press the 'c' key to create a new a new rectangle. You can drag the copy as much as you like by clicking on it once and the copy will spin in a 45 degrees angle clockwise every time you drag it. Also just in time for halloween; while your rectangle is spinning, you get halloween colors that land on a random color once you stop dragging it.

  • Every time you need a new copy, click on the red rectangle once and drag one out of it. When you run out of rectangles, click on the 'c' key again to generate more rectangles.

  • In order to delete a rectangle, you can double click on it.

  • Lastly, have fun and updates will be coming very soon.!
